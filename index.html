About Me 

I am an experienced Web Developer with a strong passion for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS. I have experience collaborating with various teams to deliver high-quality websites. My expertise also includes responsive design and web development. I have practical experience in system administration and network management.

Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in operating systems like Linux and Microsoft Windows, as well as Microsoft Hyper-V and VMWare. I am also familiarizing myself with tools such as Putty and Packet Tracer, and I have obtained certifications in these areas. Additionally, I am enthusiastic about System Development.

Fueled by these experiences I decided to launch my own freelancing brand in December 2023. Check out my website for services I offer, such as Responsive Websites, E-commerce websites, Shopify setups, and more. I'm pumped to bring my skills to the digital scene and can't wait for the adventures ahead in my web development journey.

Beyond the coding realm, you'll find me reading books, beauty (makeup & fashion) and exploring music to feed my soul. Trying out restaurants is a fun adventure where I get to enjoy different kinds of food. I love taking walks & isn't just exercise for me, it also helps clear my mind. When I need to relax, you'll often find me in my room, just chilling and thinking.

Lately, I've been getting into the concept of spirituality and culture, which adds something special to my personal journey. In this mix of community activities, being creative, discovering more about myself, and appreciating different cultures, I find inspiration that helps me grow both personally and professionally.
